Who We Are

Quiz Bowl is a team-based knowledge game. The game consists of two teams, each with four players, competing against each other for the most points. Both teams can gain points by answering questions before the other team. Topics include (but are not limited to) geography, history, mathematics, science, philosophy, mythology, and literature.

WHS Quiz Bowl is a club dedicated to practicing Quiz Bowl in teams and as individuals. We compete at local tournaments created for anyone from complete beginners to competitive teams.
